[[brandtDesigningExploratoryDesign2006]] describes these as Games to conceptualize Designing. With reference to [[1980? Habraken - Concept Design Games]] Aim is to study how designers manipulate and transform complex configurations, while making agreements and rules to how to go about their work. 

 [[1980? Habraken - Concept Design Games]] mentions 9 games that create configurations using game pieces on a game board. Neither of these resemble real-life artifacts. 

-> Games here deal with configurations using Game [[Pieces]] on a [[Game Board]].
-> [[Pieces]] are abstracted and stylized to eliminate functional knowledge and experience designers usually bring to their work (Funktionales Alltagswissen ausschalten um Fokus komplett auf Verhandlung von Strukturen, Dominanzen und Territorien zu lenken)
-> Purpose is to study structures, rules and territories

Examples 
- [[Silent Game]]
- [[Delta Game]]

Neither of these games include users. 

Purpose is to structure the design process and map out abstract concepts.
