[[brandtDesigningExploratoryDesign2006]] describes these games inspired by Surrealist Games. Their aim is to get out habits (aus Gewohnheiten ausbrechen) and embrace the unexpected. Often they start with everyday objects. Often they use elements of change. 

These were created to explore imagination and intensify collaborative experience. Often the starting points are everyday objects. They use elements of change and are based on a wish to get out of habits, see and experience new and be taken by surprise.
