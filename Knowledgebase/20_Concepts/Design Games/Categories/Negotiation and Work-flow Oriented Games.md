[[brandtDesigningExploratoryDesign2006]] describes these game with the aim to create a common understanding and language of the work context to design for. They typically explored existing use of technology and the boundaries between employees work tasks. 

Usually the participants played themselves. The primary aim is for the designers to understand existing work practice. The outcome of playing is often flow diagrams showing relations between people and tasks or tools. 

-> Aim is to create a common understanding and language of the work context to design for
-> They usually explore status quo tasks, tools and employee interactions and roles
-> Aim is also to democratically reach an agreement for requirements (worker empowerment)
