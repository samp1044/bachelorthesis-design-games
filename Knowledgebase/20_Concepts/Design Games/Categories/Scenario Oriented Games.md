[[brandtDesigningExploratoryDesign2006]] describes it as a scenario describes a particular interpretation of a use situation, but being deliberately incomplete it is also open for negotiation and change. 

According to brandt enacted scenario construction can be viewed as [[Exploratory Design Games]] because it involves a play with props, takes place within a pre-defined location, is limited in game and follows specific rules. 

Inspiration from [[Forum Theatre]]. Users, other stakeholders can be players, audience or both. And from [[The magic if]]

[[brandtDesigningExploratoryDesign2006]] states that often focus is on the functionality of the artefact to be designed and the fit within the context of use. 

-> Users or other stakeholders enact scenarios. Often [[As-if-world]]s
-> Simple Props can be used as Game [[Pieces]] (e.g. a box as a new device)
-> Aim is to generate empathy for the user and to interactively try out design ideas as well as create new ones during play
