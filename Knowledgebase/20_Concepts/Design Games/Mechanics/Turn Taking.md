[[eriksenTakingDesignGames2014]] mentions that turn taking of players is often used with the aim of equalising power [[Equalizing Power Relations in Participatory Design]]. It divides attention among the human players p. 107).

In [[UrbanTransition Game]] people take turns being responsible for a new controversy board. By starting to give points when beginning a round, and by finishing the round by placing the proposal tokens on the game board area with the final points that the group agrees on. 

[[brandtDesigningExploratoryDesign2006]] also mentions turn taking as equalizing participants.
