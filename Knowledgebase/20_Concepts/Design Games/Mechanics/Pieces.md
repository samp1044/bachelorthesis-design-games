Game pieces and artefacts to play with. 

[[brandtDesigningExploratoryDesign2006]] mentions that while playing a game the game pieces are used as "things-to-think" with. They function as [[Boundary Objects]]

- Images and video snippets from field work provide deeper knowledge of users and use situations, which designers remember
- Open-ended fragments or questions leads to a more open and creative dialogue
- Stylized pieces seem to make the participants intentions and interests clearer as they are not implicit in the materials provided [[Concept Design Games]]
