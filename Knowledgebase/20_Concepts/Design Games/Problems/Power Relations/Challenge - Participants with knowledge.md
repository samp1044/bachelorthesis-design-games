In [[eriksenTakingDesignGames2014]] while playing [[UrbanTransition Game]] a participant displays knowledge about current maintenance cost of a park while discussing. He demonstrates power as "a person that has experience". Eriksen argues that "these kinds of arguments can be very difficult to contest if one does not know the field". That expertise can be seen as professional [[Attachments]]. Eriksen also describes that further people with different expertise could bring arguments that counter-argue ones initial view.
