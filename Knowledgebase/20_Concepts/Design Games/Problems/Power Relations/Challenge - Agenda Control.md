Who decides what topics are included in the discussion? [[bratteteigDisentanglingPowerDecisionmaking2012]] describes it as one of 4 mechanisms of power: "Agenda control (what is discussed and who decides the themes)"
### Example
In [[UrbanTransition Game]] [[Challenge - Gamemasters]] preselect 6 of 12 possible controversy-pieces and their ordering. Participants as described in [[eriksenTakingDesignGames2014]] feel manipulated by this preselection. 
#### Reason
Time is stated as reason for the preselection, despite the Game allowing for a collaborative selection of which controversy-pieces to include.
#### Remedies
- Collaborative selection of the pieces to include
