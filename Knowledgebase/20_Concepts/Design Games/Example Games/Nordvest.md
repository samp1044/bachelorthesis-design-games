Example of a [[Exchange Perspective Games]] [[Exploratory Design Games]] described by [[brandtDesigningExploratoryDesign2006]]. 

Created to learn about local peoples likes, dislikes, aspirations, etc. in order to improve a public space in Copenhagen. 
#### Roles
- Gamemasters (Designers of the Game)
- Passerbys (Players)
#### Materials
- 70 images from the region
- A big dice with 6 questions, e.g. "If you should image for Nordvest what image(s) would you choose?" (Element of [[Chance]])

The images were placed face-down. Players were people passing by accident. Each player uncovers 10 images and then rolls the dice for a question. The images should then be used as inspiration and help to answer the question. 

Aim of the game was to spark dialogue with the inhabitants. While playing the students asked further questions.
