Described in [[brandtDesigningExploratoryDesign2006]]

Used to create a context for [[Persona]]s created from the [[User Game]]. Task is to create as-if-landscapes ([[As-if-world]]) for the persons highlighting physical surroundings or elements that augment activities in the persons everyday life. 

This so created [[As-if-world]] can then be enacted with [[Scenario Oriented Games]]
#### Material
- Moment cards from [[User Game]]
- Trace-Cards [[Pieces]]   (Pictures from the field material illustrating parts of the physical surrounding)
- [[Game Board]] e.g. for specific office-layouts (still abstract, like concentric circles) with labels (e.g. "important things in the middle")

#### Mechanics
- [[Turn Taking]]
- [[Pieces]]
- [[Discussion]]

Players chose a [[Game Board]] then decide which [[Pieces]] to include. Taking turns players place images of Users, Moment-cards and Trace-cards on the game-board while discussing the various configurations that emerged. The Game finishes when the players agree on the configuration created.

#### Categories
- Mainly [[Scenario Oriented Games]] 
	- Previous User workflows are staged and enacted in an office organization scenario
- Secondly [[Concept Design Games]]
	- Stylized and Abstract [[Game Board]]
	- Players place [[Pieces]] in [[Turn Taking]] to place abstract ideas from their head into negotiable structures on the Board
- Also partly [[Negotiation and Work-flow Oriented Games]]
	- Democratic participation through [[Pieces]] and [[Turn Taking]]
	- Uncovers organizational Dependencies and interest conflicts
