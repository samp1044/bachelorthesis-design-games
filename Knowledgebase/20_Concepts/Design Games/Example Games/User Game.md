As described in [[brandtDesigningExploratoryDesign2006]]
#### Materials
- Moment-[[Cards]]: Printed frame from video recordings
  Numbers on it associated the frame with the according video
- About 30 Sign-[[Cards]]: One word / card 
#### Roles
- Players - Consisting of designers and users to design for
#### Mechanics
- [[Turn Taking]]

Preparing to the game field work is conducted and the users interviewed / observed at work and/or outside work. Everything is recorded on video. 

3-5 People make a group. First the group watches and discusses the video snippets. Each player is then dealt a part of the moment cards. The first player then builds a story out of at least five Moment-cards by placing them in a line at the table. The second player builds another such story, starting by placing a Sign-card to create a label for the story first. The Stories must have one Moment card that is shared with an already laid out story.
