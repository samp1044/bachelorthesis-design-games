As described in [[brandtDesigningExploratoryDesign2006]] and [[brandtFormattingDesignDialogues2008]]. Intention is to help designers and users develop a shared image of the intended users grounded in field data. 

End result is similar to a [[Persona]] but the people using it later participate in the process of creating it. 
#### Materials
- Moment-[[Cards]]: Printed frame from video recordings
  Numbers on it associated the frame with the according video
  20-40 pieces. 
- About 30 Sign-[[Cards]]: One word / card 
  Can be specifically selected for the design context
#### Roles
- Players - Consisting of designers and users to design for
#### Mechanics
- [[Turn Taking]]
#### Outcome
- [[Persona]] like description
#### Category (Brandt)
- Mainly [[Exchange Perspective Games]]
	- Starting from real things (Videos) they get combined to create something new (Surrealist)
	- Combining as a crossword -> Getting out of habits
- Partly [[Negotiation and Work-flow Oriented Games]]
	- Create shared image of user
	- Democracy of design (through [[Game Rules]] like [[Turn Taking]] and the use of [[Pieces]] a [[Challenge - Dominant Group Member]] is prevented)

Preparing to the game field work is conducted and the users interviewed / observed at work and/or outside work for a few days. Everything is recorded on video. 

3-5 People make a group. First the group watches and discusses the video snippets. Each player is then dealt a part of the moment cards. The first player then builds a story out of at least five Moment-cards by placing them in a line at the table. The second player builds another such story, starting by placing a Sign-card to create a label for the story first. The Stories must have one Moment card that is shared with an already laid out story. 

Several rounds are played until the participants agree that new stories no langer add any information rendering the created user image as "saturated".
