### Game Material
#### Standard material
- Six out of twelve Controversy Game Board Pieces (Setting a topic for dialogue and negotiations)
- Three Game Board Sharp Corners (further concept developments)
- Middle Game Board Triangle (renegotiating by betting)
- Personal tokens to argue / bett with
- Clock for structuring time
- Turn taking figure
- Score/documentation cards - 1 per proposal
#### Scenario specific material
- 2 named proposal tokens
- 2 content specific proposal descriptions 
- 2 drawings of the proposals

The game is played in rounds. One person seems to be in Charge of a Controversy Game Board during a round. The Game is based upon comparing two or more proposals and giving them points.
