[[brandtDesigningExploratoryDesign2006]]

Aim is to develop empathy for the users and situations of use (of the design)
And to design while acting out scenarios. 

Normally together with the user. Users asked to enact scenarios in their own environment. 
Game materials are often stylized artifacts. 

Brandt stays having users enact scenarios in their own environment seems to put them on more equal footing with the design team. 

Using actors is not as productive as users as they have no stake by taking part in a design project.
