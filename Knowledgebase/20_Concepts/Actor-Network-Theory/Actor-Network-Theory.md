Highlights the importance of taking both humans and non-humans (game materials, game rules, agendas) seriously. Developed as a critique of academic social science practices. [[eriksenTakingDesignGames2014]] 

Core ANT concepts are laid out by Bruno Latour ([[latourReassemblingSocialIntroduction2005]])

Both humans and non-humans have agency and therefore can be **actors**, but not all are actors per se. Some just play intermediary roles. Some actors are also **mediators**, making others do unexpected things. [[latourReassemblingSocialIntroduction2005]] 

[[latourReassemblingSocialIntroduction2005]] suggests seeking relations in **networks**, especially with a focus on mediators and their **attachments**

For example in [[UrbanTransition Game]] Controversy board pieces become mediating actors.
