[[eriksenTakingDesignGames2014]] states that [[kensingParticipatoryDesignIssues1998]] define Participatory Design largely as _"an effort to rebalance the power relations between users and technical experts and between workers and managers"_-

[[brandtDesigningExploratoryDesign2006]] mentions that participatory design implies active involvement of the people designed for and other stakeholders of the design work. Brandt also mentions that many authors have used the game metaphor as a way of understanding/organising participation.
