[[brandtDesigningExploratoryDesign2006]] references Cooper who has introduced "Persona" as a technique to describe and communicate users to others. 
#### Criticism
(From [[brandtDesigningExploratoryDesign2006]])
- Personas are typically constructed by someone else (a usability specialist or a team of consultants) and after that handed over to the design team
- Discussions often happen between steps and not as part of a step
- Users that are designed for are not part of creating personas
- Content is focused on work related areas with only little personal details added
- Often chosen lists are not the best format for memorizing the persona long-term

Persona creation also seems to suffer from [[Challenge - Dominant Group Member]]
